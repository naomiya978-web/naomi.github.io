Fly Me To The Moon

This is a Puzzle Adventure game. The player will take on the role of a lost traveler, trapped in a rift in time and space. The player must find keys in different rooms to unlock the door to the next level. Then, reaching the end and escaping the rift.

Unreal Engine 5
